About Pfefferkorn Assisted Living

Pfefferkorn Assisted Living is a senior care facility located at 3320 Pfefferkorn Road in West Friendship, Maryland. We are licensed to care for up to 5 residents, allowing us to provide personalized attention and a family-like atmosphere.

Our facility is designed to offer a comfortable, home-like environment where seniors can maintain their independence while receiving the support they need.

Small, intimate setting with personalized care
Experienced, compassionate staff
Affordable rates compared to county and state averages

Our Services & Amenities

Personal Care Assistance

Our staff provides compassionate assistance with daily living activities including bathing, dressing, eating, and toileting. We also help with transfers using walkers, wheelchairs, and lifts.

Nutritious Meals

We serve three delicious, home-cooked meals daily with options to accommodate various dietary needs and preferences. Our dining experience promotes social interaction and enjoyment.

Transportation Services

Scheduled transportation is available for medical appointments, shopping trips, and social outings, helping residents stay connected with the community.

Comfortable Amenities

Our facility features wheelchair-friendly common areas, a barber and beauty shop, internet access, and a library for residents' enjoyment and convenience.

Social Activities

We organize regular social events, games, and activities to keep residents engaged, stimulated, and connected with one another.

Outdoor Spaces

Enjoy our pleasant outdoor areas designed for relaxation and socialization, with comfortable seating and beautiful surroundings.
